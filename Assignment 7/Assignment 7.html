Hello applicant,

This will help you apply for the Vickery Scholarship. Here are the rules:

  1. You must be 18 or older (when applying).
  2. Must have a school you're wanting to attend.
  3. Have a valid phone number and email (so if selected, we are able to inform you of your Scholarship).
  4. Maintained a 3.0 or higher GPA.
  5. Maintain attendance according to your selected school's attendance policy.
